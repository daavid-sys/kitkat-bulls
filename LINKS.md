## Reonic Track — Key APIs & Data

- **Google Maps Solar API** - https://mapsplatform.google.com/maps-products/solar/
  - Provides solar potential data, roof segment geometry, sunshine hours, and financial analysis for buildings
  - [API docs](https://developers.google.com/maps/documentation/solar/overview)
- **Google 3D Tiles API** - https://developers.google.com/maps/documentation/tile/3d-tiles
  - Photogrammetry 3D models of buildings — useful for roof detection and module placement
  - Costs will be refunded by Reonic
- **Reonic dataset** - provided by Reonic (real residential energy systems with inputs and final designs)

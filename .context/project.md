# Project: AI Renewable Designer

## One-liner
Self-serve solar proposal tool: homeowner enters address → their actual house becomes an interactive 3D proposal with panels auto-placed on the roof → instant cost/ROI with voice narration.

## Who we're building for
- **Reonic** — a CRM for solar panel installers. We're building a feature for their platform.
- **End user 1: Homeowner** — enters address, gets a self-serve proposal without talking to anyone
- **End user 2: Solar installer** — reviews AI-generated proposals, adjusts, approves, sends final offer

## The problem
Designing renewable energy systems is the slowest part of the installation process. Every home is different. Installers spend 25-35 minutes per proposal manually tracing roofs, placing panels, selecting components, and generating PDFs. Time that could be spent on actual installations.

## Our solution
An AI-native flow that collapses 17 manual steps into ~2 minutes of self-serve:
1. Homeowner types address
2. Their actual house appears in 3D (Google 3D Tiles / photogrammetry)
3. AI auto-detects roof, places panels, selects components
4. Instant proposal: cost, savings/year, break-even, 25-year ROI
5. Voice narration walks them through it (Gradium)
6. Homeowner refines, then submits to installer or signs digitally
